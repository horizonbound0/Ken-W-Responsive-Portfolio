About Me:

Born to a military family, I've called many places home, but am currently based in Austin, Texas. I've loved creating experiences and content for people to enjoy ever since I was a child. Being older than THE INTERNET, I've always been facinated with how experessive and engaging the world-wide-web can be. With a bachelor's in Video Game Design and completion of an intense Full Stack Web Developement bootcamp from the University of Texas, Austin, I'm incredibly excited to leave my stamp in browsers across the world.
Take a look at my works and projects below. You'll find that I strive to create a comfortable, accessible, and fun experience for my users. I firmly believe these traits can be successfully applied to any and all corners of the internet. I look forward to hearing from you.
